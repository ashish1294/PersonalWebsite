  • A Short Autobiography

    I am 21 years old. I am tall (about ~ 187cm) but I weigh about 128 lbs (58 kgs). My eyesight is a little weak mostly because of the long hours I spend in-front of display terminals. First thing, I do not like being social - mostly because I do not know what it exactly means! I belong to a very diverse Indian joint family of more than 20 people. I like music + hot cappuccino + black / green terminal and that is why I am often seen with all three of it. Like most engineers in India, I usually sleep late at night. I like watching movies, music videos, TV Shows, Animes and Documentaries. Apart from Computer Science, I am also interested in particle physics. My Primary Gods include - David Gilmour, Stephen Hawking, Steve Jobs and Alan Turing.

    My failures have motivated me more than my achievements. I work as hard as possible. I am usually good at working under pressure with an upcoming deadline to meet. I also like self-learning usually via web. I hate reading books - novels, technical, academic, etc - all of it. I am not patient enough to read a whole book. I love debating especially technical debate among a small set of people. I am rarely angry. I am a vegetarian and like trying out new dishes and cuisine. I maintain a digital diary and occasionally write stuff. I have traveled through 21/29 states in India. I have visited 2 other countries apart from India i.e., UAE and US.

    I grew up in Kolkata, India along with a number of cousins. So my childhood was a happy one (:P). I was only an above-average student at my primary school. I was always good with Mathematics. After primary education, I attended Birla High School - one of the best schools in Eastern India. It played a pivotal role in my academic career. However, I failed to get a branch of my choice in any of the IITs and thus I joined NITK Surathkal. I made a lot of friends here. I learn a lot here - everyday. I took up a couple of leadership roles too. Currently, I am in my senior year. I try to learn from everyone here. I also try to help others as much as possible. I lived in Mumbai for a couple of months - which was awesome. I also went to San Francisco to intern at Google during summer 2015 - which by far is the most prominent achievement of my life.

    I use ashish1294 as the username / handle for most of my web accounts. It represents my date of birth i.e., 1294 = 1/2/94.
